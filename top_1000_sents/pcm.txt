Afta wey e marej wit Kenneth Robertson skata in 1934 , Sidney pak go New York City an e hep daret di Sosha Muzik Program at di 'Henry Street Settlement '' on di lowa East side .
E wok wit one eldali Jewish wuman .
Sidney wok as Assistant for Charles Seeger in 1936 .
Charles Seeger na di teknikal advaisa of di yunit of di Spesha Skills Divishon of di Risetumet Administrashon in Washington ( dey leta chenj di nem to Farm Sekihoriti Administrashon ) .
Na wen e stat to wok as assistant e dey kolet an rekod tradishonal muzik in Appalachia , di Ozarks an di hopa Midwest .
Sidney leta go bak to Kalifornia wey e stat an arenj mek di Librari of Kongress , di Yunivasiti of Kalifornia , Berkeley an di Wok Progress Administrashon of di Norta Kalifornia Folk Muzik Projet sponsor e projet wey wan du .
Sidney kom bi di daretor of di projet from 1938 go rish 1940 .
Na 20 wokas dey wok onda am dat time .

Leda Valladares ( dey bon am on December 21 , 1919 an e kpai July 13 , 2012 ) na Argentina singa , song-raita , pesin wey rid muzik an pesin way dey stodi an rekod how dem dey du tins for lokal way .
Dey bon Leda Valladares in San Miguel de Tucumán in Norta Argentina .
Valladares gro up lisinin to old European muzik an tradishonal song of Amerindian pipul wey dey live for di aria .
Wen Valladares dey smol , e len how to play Piano .
Valladares wit e broda form one muzikal grup wey dey pratis tradishonal song , jazz an kul muzik or blues .
Valladares dey poblish puem til e kpai .
E fest stat e yunivasiti stodis in Nashonal Yunivasiti of Tucumán wen e rid Inglish , Valladares Afta one year chenj klass to filosofi an edukeshon .
In 1948 , e finish yunivasiti .
As Valladares dey rid filosofi an edukeshon for skul , e stil chuk eye for muzik stodis in Akedemi of Fine Art an in Indipendet risach among pipul wey dey sing tradishonal muzik .
Afta wey Valladares finish skul , e fest tish for somtime bifor e pak go Paris in di heli 1950s .
For Paris , e form one muzik grup wit Maria Elena Walsh .
Both of dem dey sing tradishonal Argentina song for wey pipul dey drink an for tihata .
Di grup go bak to Argentina Afta Juan Peron komot as presidor bikos of Libiretin Revolushon wey happun in 1956 .
For dat time , pipul kom like Argentina tradishonal muzik smol an Valladares grup dwy mek albom an sing dat time .
Pipul wey kom dey lisin an enjoi dia muzik no plenty like dat .
Valladares an e sekod wey form di grup Maria Elena Walsh kom skata an go dia way in 1962 .
Valladares kom stat to dey kolet an rekod di tradishonal old song of Argentina .
E mek some albom from di koleshon an rikodin wey e du from 1960 go rish 1974 .
Di nem of di albom na : Mapa musical de la Argentina ( Musical Map of Argentina ) .
Di albom rikod an kip meni old tradishonal song wey dey di kontri from Doreen difren lokeshon .

Di time wey dem dey du EndSARS for e stet , dey rekod for live TV wey e tok sey di LSHA no go fit For di time wey dem dey du EndSARS for im stet , wey be Legos dem rekod am for live TV wey e say di LSHA no go fit konfam di pipu wey dai for polis hand wen dem dey du di one minit silence for di pipul wey dem kil for Lekki an oda pipul for Naijá .
Bot e no gri wit wetin dem put for e hed .
Ripot pipul kom leta sey dat Economic and Financial Crimes Commission kari Obasa to ask am som tins for 8 and 9 Octorber 2020 .
Fom wetin inside pesin tel di Sahara pipul , dem say Obasa du like pesin wey no wel for di EFCC wen dem dey ask am som kwesions , an dis mek dem stop to ask am oda tins .
Dem nid to Kari Obasa go dia sick bay bifor dey kom free am .
Obasa kom ask for im passport for am to go for tritment for obodo Oyinbo bifor e go du Umrah wia e mit Bola Tinubu for Saudi Arabia .
Obasa dey onda alligeshon by di EFCC for Kes of moni magomago .

Tajudeen Abbas ( dem bon am for 1 October 1963 ) na Naijà politishian an na im bi di niu 15 Spika of di haus of Representatives for Naijà sins 2023 .
E get Bachelor 's digre in biznes Administrashion an Master 's fom Ahmadu Bello Univasiti , Zaria .
E leta get doctorate digre in biznes Administrashion fom Usmanu Danfodiyo Univasiti Sokoto .
E leta kon wok as lecturer for one Polytechnic .
Na lecturer for Kaduna stet Univasiti fom 1983 to 2001 .
E wok for compini sef as Marketing Manager for Nigerian Tobacco Distribution Company , wey don chenj to British American Tobacco Company .
In 2010 e join politis an na e dey insaid pipu wey dey Haus of Representative in 2011 .
Abbas na king pikin fom Zazzau Emirates , Zaria , Kaduna Stet .
Na im stat lor wey plenti pas in di 8th Assemblies an e get rekod web e stat 74 bills insaid 21 wey dem sign insaid lor for 2019 an 2023 .
E wok for difren committee for di haus lik Commerce , Finance , Speshial Duties , Defence , Public Procurement , an Nashonal planning an Economic Development Committee .
Bifor e enta for spika , na im bi di Haus Committee Chairman for Land transport .
Abbas enta as spika of di 10th Assembli wit 353 votes fom 359 votes .
